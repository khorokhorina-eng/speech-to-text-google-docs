Privacy Policy

How Speech to Text for Google Docs will process your data.

This service processes account, usage, and billing data to support dictation limits and paid access.

What the extension does

  • Runs speech recognition while the user dictates in Google Docs.
  • Sends product data to the product backend once the final domain is configured.
  • Stores a device token and limited billing state to manage free usage, subscriptions, and purchases.
  • Uses Google sign-in before checkout so a paid plan can be attached to the account.

What data is processed

  • Recognition transcripts inserted into Google Docs while the extension is active.
  • A device token used to identify usage limits and subscription state.
  • Billing-related identifiers when a user starts or manages a paid plan through Stripe.
  • Support request details if the user contacts support.

How data is used

  • To support speech-to-text dictation inside Google Docs.
  • To enforce free usage limits and paid subscription rules.
  • To process payments and subscription access through Stripe.
  • To respond to support requests.

Third parties

  • Stripe for checkout, billing, and subscription management.
  • Google OAuth for account sign-in before checkout.

Data sale
